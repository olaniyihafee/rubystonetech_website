Perimeter intrusion detection systems

We design outdoor Perimeter Intrusion Detection Systems for premises which is most exposed to the outside (gardens, walls, fences etc), ensuring security all along the line surrounding the premises. Our detection system are integrated with other electronic security systems and ensures the monitoring and recording of potential threat. The cameras automatically turn towards the hint of intrusion. Our system archives the images and other data and alarms for eventual retrospective investigations separately for each operator.
